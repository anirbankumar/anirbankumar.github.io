Blockr & Blockr Plus

Released in 2015

These were my first two apps and they block ads on iOS' Safari. Blockr Plus is an extension to Blockr and also blocks YouTube ads. I removed Blockr Plus in the beginning of 2020.


SAHRI

Released in 2016

SAHRI was an app I voluntarily made for a non profit orgnization. It's the official app for the South Asian Health Research Institute. Through this app, we aim to get people information about various health topics that effect South Asians quickly.


Dodgy Ninja

Released in 2016

Granted it's no Fortnite, but this was the first game I made. It's fairly simple code and logic wise, but I learned a lot. The objective is to make sure the Ninja doesn't run into any walls by tapping the screen.


Basketball Keeper

Released in 2016

Keep track of your basketball game with ESPN-like detailed statistics. Track points, fouls, shots to create a detailed game report, which you can email to yourself as a PDF. This app was featured as an App Store Story here.


Locate It AR

Released in 2019

AR Locator allows you to pin locations and view them using AR. It's like an AR compass that can help you find your parked car in a crowded parking lot or view other places, such as famous landmarks.


InstaMusic

Won't be releasing it, but it's available on my GitHub.

InstaMusic allows Apple Music users to easily share what they're listening to to Instagram Stories as a nice image.



HWDYKM

Should be available by May 2020.

HWDYKM, which stands for How Well Do You Know Me, is an app that allows friends and families to take quizzes to see how well the know each other. There is a generic quiz with preset questions or users can make their own custom quiz with up to 10 questions.
